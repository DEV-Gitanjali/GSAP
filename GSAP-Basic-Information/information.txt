What is Animation  ?

-> Animation is the process of showing a series of images or changes in objects over time to create the illusion of movement.
In simple words, animation makes things move or change smoothly on the screen.
-> When something changes from one state to another with motion, like moving, rotating, fading, growing, bouncing — that is animation.

It can be small (like a button changing color) or big (like a cartoon character walking or a car moving).
